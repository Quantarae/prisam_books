Overview

Welcome to PriSam Books, a storytelling application designed for children and families. Your privacy and the safety of your children are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information Collection

PriSam Books does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • We do not require account creation or registration.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.
  • The app operates primarily offline for a safe and controlled environment.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Third-Party Services

We may use standard mobile industry services that collect basic, non-identifying technical data to improve app performance:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and security.
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand app crashes and performance (Non-personal data only).

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with app usage data. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
